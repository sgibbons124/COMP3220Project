Welcome to the Hobbiton University Library. Established in the Third Age (TA) 1601, Hobbiton University is located near Ivy Bush at the eastern end of the village, just a short distance west of Bywater. While small, Hobbiton University offers several degree programs including a wide range of classes and learning opportunities for Hobbits of all ages. Feel free to search through the many books in our library or login to manage your account.
